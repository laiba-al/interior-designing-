About Us

Our Story

Welcome to our new website! We are dedicated to providing the best services and products to our customers. Our journey started with a passion for innovation and a commitment to excellence. Here's a bit more about who we are and what we do:

The journey of Dream True Interior Design is a story of vision, perseverance, and a relentless pursuit of excellence. Our founder and CEO, Laiba Ali, laid the foundation of Dream True with a passion for creating spaces that inspire and elevate. Laiba's vision was to build a company that not only delivers outstanding interior design but also fosters a culture of collaboration, innovation, and integrity.


Laiba Ali's journey began with her graduation from a prestigious design school abroad, where she honed her skills and broadened her horizons. Her international education provided her with a unique perspective on design, blending global trends with local sensibilities. Armed with this rich educational background and a profound love for design, Laiba set out to make a mark in the industry.


Under Laiba's dynamic leadership, Dream True has grown from a small start-up to a renowned name in the interior design world. Her unwavering dedication to excellence and her ability to inspire her team have been pivotal to the company's success. Laiba believes in the power of teamwork and is known for her hands-on approach, working closely with her team to ensure every project is executed to perfection.
